Using the Appliances

To ensure that the appliances continue to serve your needs and optimise your lifestyle without interuptions, do take good care when using it. It is highly recommended to read the manual for correctly using the appliances.The quick guide is only intended to assist for when you fist move in and dont have much time on hand.

Appliances

Toshiba Dishwasher DW-08T1(S)

Quick Guide

  • Ensure main power and water outlet is turned on --> turn on machine power [1]
  • Arrange cleared dirty plates/utensils inside
  • Put in detergent [A] and add dishwashing finish liquid [B] (no need salt as malaysia uses soft water)
  • Select program P1-P6 [2], then press start [4]
  • Download manual
  • watch youtube video

Electrolux Microwave Oven EMS2085X

Quick Guide to Reheat Food

  • Ensure main power switch is on at the power panel above the dinner table
  • Turn power knob [2] to select desired power level
  • Turn the setting knob [3] to set the desired time.
  • Press start [4]

Quick Guide
