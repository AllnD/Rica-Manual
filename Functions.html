There is ample storage to ensure that the movement flow (blue arrows) and functional spaces shown in the diagram will never be cluttered, thus ensuring a pleasant living environment much efficient than bigger units. As espoused and made popular by Marie Kondo, items should have a purpose or add value to your life, and has its own designated storage space so that it can be returned each time after use.The list below provides a guide on some suggested functional areas that might help in daily living.

Functional Aspects Around the House

Foyer Entrance Cabinetry

This space can be used to store cleaning equipment and has a power point for charging standing vacuum cleaners. It also has a broom handle holder.

Charging your vacuum cleaner, using broom holder and hooks

  • To use the broom holder, lift the holder upwards and slide the broom handle in.

Decorative light

  • The decorative light needs to be charged periodically via USB cable. Please use the step-ladder to retrieve the light. This step ladder is used to reach higher storage spaces throughout the house. Be careful when using it.
  • Please note that in order for the light remote to work, the light's sensors need to be facing up towards the ceiling. The remote's signal is aimed towards the ceiling and bounced towards the light's sensors to activate.

Multipurpose Area 1

This area has a large quartz table top with numerous power points. It can be used as:

  • Dinner table and coffee station
  • Meeting or conference area during work from home with the glass wall as a discussion board. Do remember to use water-based marker pens.
  • Additional Food preparation countertop.
  • Additional study or workspace.

Multipurpose Area 2

  • This area can used as extra workspace or space to store frequent used appliances like rice cookers.
  • This area can also be used to place any water dispensers. It is recommended to take a picture of the piping inlet and send it to the technician so that they can arrange the best installation methods.

Mood Lighting in Living Room

The ceiling lights in the living hall can be adjusted to 3 different brightness levels by switching it on and off in sequence. This will allow the right ambience for various activities in the night such as watching movies or having dinner.

Storage Bed Frame

The bed frame can be opened to reveal more storage space. It is recommended to store bulky items which are not often used. To open with mattress on top, lift both sides simultaneously. To close, repeat the process by pressing down. You might need 2 person to close as it needs more strenght to press down on the hydrolics.

Outdoor Clothes Hanger

There is a small retractable clothes hanger in the balcony. When and if needed to dry by natural sun and air, pull out the clothes hanger to use. Push it back in after use.

Natural ventilation to clean the air inside living hall

Due to the small unit size, at certain times, during heavier cooking, it is important to ensure air which has a cooking oil smell do not linger within the main living area. Other than using the cooking hood, to further clear the air, follow the steps below:

  • Slightly open the main front door
  • Switch on the ventilation fan.
  • Open the main sliding door.

There will be a strong wind draft each time this is done , thus quickly improving air circulation in the unit. Standing at the door entrance will confirm this as you feel the breeze.
